MANCALA

■ Insert Coin ■ Sow & Capture ■

Sowing runs counter-clockwise → toward your own store. Bottom row is South’s, top row is North’s.

Pick a non-empty pit on your side and sow its stones one-by-one counter-clockwise into the following pits and your own store, skipping your opponent’s store. Land the last stone in your store and you go again. Land it in an empty pit on your side and you capture it plus every stone in the pit directly opposite. When one side empties, the other player banks the rest — most stones in store wins.
