Gear Checklist

Always Bring

  • Plenty of water (at least 1L per hour of hiking in hot weather)
  • Sun protection: hat, sunscreen SPF 50+, sunglasses
  • Sturdy hiking boots with ankle support
  • First aid kit with blister supplies
  • Headlamp with extra batteries
  • Offline maps (downloaded before you lose signal)
  • Snacks / trail food
  • Rain jacket (weather changes fast in the mountains)
