Sand Garden — quick help
  • Paint: click/drag the canvas. Fills only empty cells (eraser clears anything).
  • Select: palette buttons or their keys (shown on hover). 1-0 and letters.
  • Speed: drag/scroll the little dial — 0.1× slow-mo to 3× fast.
  • Watch the reactions: everything talks to its neighbors. Fire, lightning, plants, void, etc create chains.
  • Tip: C clears walls too. Build new barriers with brick/wood/stone if you want to contain the fun.
